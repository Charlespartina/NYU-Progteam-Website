About NYU Progteam

Our team's mission is to enjoy programming by solving algorithmic problems and learning blazingly fast algorithm implementations.

We host weekly onsite practice contests every Friday night, with refreshements. No matter you are a programming veteran or beginner, just bring your laptop and come, you will always benefit from our weekly meetings.

We annually send teams to the ACM-ICPC competitions.

Please sign up for our mailing list, and discussion board, for future announcements.
